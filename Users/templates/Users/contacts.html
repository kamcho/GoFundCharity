Our Mission

Our mission is to create positive change in the lives of those in need by connecting them with support from individuals and organizations. We strive to build long-lasting relationships that foster a more compassionate and equitable society.


What is GoFundKE ?

We are a Kenyan-based charity, committed to connecting people in need with those who are willing to help. Our mission is to bridge the gap between those who are less fortunate and those who can lend a helping hand. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to identify people in need and connect them with individuals or organizations who can provide support, creating long-lasting relationships for a brighter future. We are proud to work alongside the local community to create positive change and a more compassionate society.
